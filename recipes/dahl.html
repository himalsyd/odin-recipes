Dahl

Description

Dahl is lentils soup

As most of our dishes that goes with rice dry, dahl comes in as a soup to add to the meal

Ingredients

  • Lentils
  • Garlic
  • Chilli
  • salt
  • Oil
  • Tomato

How to cook

  1. Heat a little oil in the pan
  2. Add 1 chopped chilli, garlic and fry until brown
  3. Add lentils and add water
  4. Once lentil has softed and water has become thick, add little tumeric and half a tomato
  5. Once the consistency is thick and tomato has dissolved, its time to serve
