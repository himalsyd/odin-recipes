Ema Datshi

Description

Ema Datshi is considered to the Bhutanese people's favorite dish therefore also called the National dish

It can be literally translated as Chilli(Ema) and Cheese(Datshi)

It is an easy dish to prepare with minimal ingredients which is a key aspect of Bhutanese cuisine

Ingredients

  • Chillies
  • Cheese
  • Cooking Oil
  • Salt
  • Garlic

Cooking Steps

  1. Cut chilles lenght wise and add it to a pan
  2. Peel 2-3 garlic pieces, cut in half and add it to the pan
  3. Add a little water
  4. Add salt to taster
  5. Add 2 table spoons on oil on the top
  6. Top it with cheese (any cheese) and cover the pan with a lid
  7. Let it cook in a medium heat until cheese melts
  8. Once all cheese is melted, open the lid and stir well
  9. Ready to serve
